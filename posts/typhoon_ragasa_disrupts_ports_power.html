Typhoon Ragasa Disrupts Ports and Power Grids

Trigger risk: Elevated Typhoon Disruption SHIPPING Confidence: Medium Score: 70

What changed

Typhoon Ragasa struck southern China and Taiwan, causing flooding, barrier‑lake burst, and outages to 56,000+ households in Guangdong. Port operations were suspended in coastal hubs.

Channels of stress

  • Trade flow interruptions and shipment delays.
  • Electric grid stress in coastal industrial zones.

Watch next

  • Return of port activity or persistent shutdowns.
  • Supply chain defaults or reroutes.
  • Freight rate spikes on Asia‑Europe/U.S. routes.
Sources: Reuters reports on Ragasa landfall and flooding
