Typhoon Ragasa Triggers Port & Power Disruptions

Trigger risk: Elevated Typhoon Disruption SHIPPING Confidence: Medium Score: 68

What changed

Super Typhoon Ragasa made landfall across Guangdong, Guangxi, and later Vietnam, causing power outages to 56,000+ households and triggering mass evacuations.

Impacts & channels

  • Port closures and shipping delays are confirmed in Guangdong coastal hubs.
  • Energy disruption adds stress to industrial and logistics sectors.

Watch next
