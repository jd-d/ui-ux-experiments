Playbook for a late-cycle break

This framework blends structural signals (demographics, land and credit cycles) with real-time data (liquidity, policy, risk appetite). Triggers are mapped to probability bands and suggested portfolio shifts.

Trigger tiers

  1. Pressure building: Early warning from liquidity and balance sheet data.
  2. Cracks spreading: Credit and funding disruptions move beyond niche markets.
  3. Break point: Feedback loop hits real economy and policy makers react.

Every signal is plotted against these tiers to keep the roadmap clear even when noise is high.
